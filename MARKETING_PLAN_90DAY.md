## Content Strategy

### The Two Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: "Strategy Autopsy" Series (60% of content)

Backtest and analyze popular trading strategies with brutal honesty.

**Format:**
1. Take a popular strategy (TradingView, YouTube traders, Reddit)
2. Backtest it with real data
3. Show actual win rate, drawdown, profit factor, expectancy
4. Be brutally honest ‚Äî if it sucks, say it sucks
5. End with CTA: "Want to automate a strategy that actually works? Link in bio"

**Why This Works:**
- Provides massive value (people pay consultants for this analysis)
- Positions you as the unbiased expert
- Creates controversy and engagement
- Naturally leads to "what strategies DO work?" ‚Üí Atomik marketplace
- Tagging original strategy creators gives you exposure to their audience

**Example Titles:**
- "I Backtested the Most Popular TradingView Strategy (Here's the Truth)"
- "This Reddit Trading Strategy Made $47,000... Or Did It?"
- "The Strategy Everyone Uses That Actually Loses Money"
- "I Tested 5 'Profitable' Strategies So You Don't Have To"

#### Pillar 2: "Building Atomik" Series (40% of content)

Document the journey of building the company.

**Format:**
- Weekly or bi-weekly updates
- Share real numbers (MRR, users, challenges)
- Behind-the-scenes of building features
- Creator success stories
- Vulnerable moments and lessons learned

**Why This Works:**
- Creates emotional connection with audience
- Attracts other founders/builders (potential users)
- Attracts VCs who love founder stories
- Differentiates from faceless competitors
- Endless content (just document what you're already doing)

**Example Titles:**
- "I Disappeared to Build This (Why I'm Back)"
- "Month 1: $X MRR - Here's Everything I Learned"
- "A Creator Just Made $7,500 on My Platform (Here's How)"
- "The Feature That Almost Broke Everything"
- "From 20 to 50 Users: What Actually Worked"

## Final Notes

### Mindset Reminders

1. **Consistency beats perfection.** A mediocre video posted is better than a perfect video never made.

2. **Volume is the strategy.** Hormozi's advice: "The market will tell you what it wants. You just have to give it enough options to choose from."

3. **Give away the secrets.** The more value you give for free, the more people trust you. They'll pay for convenience and implementation.

4. **Document, don't create.** You're already building Atomik. Just document the journey. Endless content.

5. **Play the long game.** Day 1-30 will feel slow. By day 60-90, compounding kicks in.

### When You Feel Stuck

- **Can't think of video ideas?** Backtest a popular strategy. Document what you did today.
- **No engagement?** Keep posting. Algorithms reward consistency over time.
- **Creators not responding?** Feature them first. Give before you ask.
- **Low conversions?** Add more proof. Stack more testimonials.
